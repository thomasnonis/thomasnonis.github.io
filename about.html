ABOUT ME

The more you know, the less you know

Since I was a child I've always been fascinated by understanding the working principles of the things that surrounded me. This brought me to studying electronics in high school and later electronics and computer science engineering at university. I was never satisfied by what was taught though, this is why I kept pursuing my passions (which I have lots of, probably too many) on the side.
The more I learn about new things, the more I understand that I actually know nothing. This is where my motto The more you know, the less you know comes from
